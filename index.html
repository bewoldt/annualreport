Through the newly implemented “Every Student, Every Encounter” initiative, Vice President for Student Life, Tom Rocklin, encourages faculty and staff to have frequent conversations with students about their academics. If the University of Iowa faculty surrounds them with a climate of high expectations, students are going to try to meet those expectations. “Every Student, Every Encounter” encourages students to pursue excellence both in and out of the classroom.





Fostering student success by creating and promoting educationally
purposeful services and activities within and beyond the classroom.
